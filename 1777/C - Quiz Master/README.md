Otherwise, he is a rookie in that topic. </p><p>We say that a team of students is collectively proficient in all the topics if for every topic there is a member of the team proficient in this topic.</p><p>Find a team that is collectively proficient in all the topics such that the maximum difference between the smartness of any two students in that team is <span class="tex-font-style-bf">minimized</span>.
